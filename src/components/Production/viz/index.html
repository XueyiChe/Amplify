The sunburst chart demonstrate the diversity of silicon valley by catagory of gender, race and job catagory.

User can put mouse over the partition to see the exact propotion of people with specific gender, race, catagory and company.

Gender chart

The gender chart show the number and percentage of gender distribution in each company.The positive part show the number/percentage of female employee in the company while the negative part show the number/percentage of male employee.
User can move the mouse on to see the exact number of each part.
number: 
As the gender chart demonstrated, the big company in silicon valley are male-dominant with large propotion of male employees.
In general, 23 and me has the best gender diversity with 50.17% female and 49.83% male.
For company with more than 5000 employee, Intuit has the best gender diversity with 42.94% female and 57.06% male.

Race chart

The race chart show the number of race distribution in each company.The stacked bar chart show the number/percentage of each race employee in the company.
As the race chart demonstrated, the big company in silicon valley are white-dominant with large propotion of white employees.
In general, Nvidia has the best race diversity with mixed race employees.
HP Inc. has the worst race diversity.

Dashboard

The dashboard includes two charts. The left bar chart show the overall race distribution in Silicon Valley, the right pie chart shows the distribution of job category.
User can move the mouse over the bar chart, the pie chart would switch to the job category distribution of the specific race as the user move the mouse on. The number and percentage of job category of the specific race would demonstrate in the table below.
Moreover, user can also move the mouse over the pie chart, the bar chart would switch to the race distribution of the specific job category as the user move the mouse on.
